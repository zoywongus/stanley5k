The race

One flat loop around Green Lake. 2.8 miles of paved path plus a short connector to make it a true 5k. Chip-timed, well-marshaled, with aid stations and finisher swag.

Distance

5 kilometers

3.1 miles on the paved Green Lake loop path. Course map posted one week before race day.

Timing

Chip-timed

Official results posted within 3 hours of the finish. Awards for the top finishers, plus additional prizes raffled off to participants.

What's included

Bib + finisher medal

All registered runners get a bib number and a finisher medal at the chute. No race shirt provided.

Vibe

Sprinters welcome

Sprinters welcome, runners tolerated, walkers tolerated. Show up, move forward, have fun.

Race fee

The entry fee to race is one canned or non-perishable food item for every marathon the runner has completed in their lifetime, donated to a local Seattle food bank or charity.

1 item × every marathon you've ever finished

Never run a marathon? One item. Five? Five items. Drop-off details and logistics will be shared with registered runners ahead of race day. This is not a race-morning drop-off.

Stanley Qualifier · SQ

A path to the Stanley Marathon

Stanley's Seattle 5k is an official Stanley Qualifier (SQ) event. Finish within your age group's SQ standard and you'll lock in an automatic bib to the famed Stanley Marathon. No lottery, no waitlist, no re-qualifying. SQ standards are calculated as a percentage of the overall race winner's time, scaled by age.

Age group · SQ standard

Qualifying performance must be verified via the official race chip-timing system. Self-reported results are not accepted. Final SQ standards are confirmed race week and may be subject to revision at the race director's discretion.

FAQ

Can't find what you're looking for? Email Stanley at stanleywongus@icloud.com.

Is there day-of registration?

Yes. Sign up at the start-line tent starting at 6:45 AM. Heads up: the day-of race fee is double the pre-registration fee, so signing up in advance saves you a chunk of change.

How do I get there / where do I park?

Parking around Green Lake is extremely limited on weekend mornings, so public transit or walking is strongly recommended. Each runner is responsible for arranging their own transportation and parking. We won't be posting specific lot or street-parking info on this site.

Please do not run-commute to the start line. Arriving on tired legs compromises the integrity of the race for everyone competing fresh, and any runner found to have run-commuted may be disqualified from official results.

Can I bring my dog / stroller?

Yes, dogs and strollers are welcome as long as dogs are leashed at all times. Please line up at the back of the pack for everyone's safety.

What about the weather?

It's Seattle, so the race proceeds rain or shine, including through typical wet, windy, or cold conditions. The only reason we'd cancel is if the National Weather Service Seattle office issues a severe weather warning (e.g. lightning, extreme heat, or other genuinely unsafe conditions) for the Green Lake area on race day. If that happens, registered runners will be notified by email as early as possible.

Can I get a refund?

Strict no-refund policy. All entries are final, including in the event of cancellation, injury, or no-show. Bibs are non-transferable.
